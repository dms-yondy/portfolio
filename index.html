About Me

Always Leaning. Always Improving. Always Coding.

Hi, my name is Rory and I am on a quest to become the best software engineer possible. In June 2020 I graduated from the University of Manchester with a 1st class Meng in Chemical Engineering. During my time at university I started to learn code as a hobby in my spare time. The more I learnt about computer science and technology the more fascinated I became. The sense of achievement and enjoyment I got from learning a new technology or algorithm to solving coding challenges on Hakerrank or Codeforce was so unique.

While finding the time to learn and improve my coding skills was not always easy while studying a demanding degree like chemical engineering, I always ending up come back to pick up where I left off in my coding journey to improve existing skills and learn new ones.

During my final summer at university I had the opportunity to go and work in the IT department of BMW Group UK. While there I was able to develop applications and websites for the business. While receiving positive feedback for my work, I was able to learn and develop, so much from the programmers and software engineers there, for example I learnt a great deal about the role of testing in software engineer and how it is implemented into projects. I was also introduced to working in an Agile environment.

Having finished university my ambition now is to find work in the tech industry as a software engineer. Every day I strive to learn more and improve, weather that is by learning new technologies, contributing to open source projects or working on my own projects, I see every day as an opportunity to learn, get better and achieve my dream career.

I have developed websites as part of my work experience and through my own projects. I have worked with various frameworks and JavaScript libraries such as Angular and React. In any spare time I have, I am always trying to learn new CSS tricks or becoming a better JavaScript programmer.
